About MOSDAC

The Meteorological and Oceanographic Satellite Data Archival Centre (MOSDAC) is a facility at Space Applications Centre (SAC), ISRO for archival, processing and dissemination of Oceanographic, Atmospheric and Land satellite data.

MOSDAC provides access to satellite data from various Indian and international satellites including INSAT, SCATSAT, Oceansat, and many others.
